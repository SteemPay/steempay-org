Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? Here are a few of the questions that we get a lot. Of course, we're happy to answer any question you may have!

Is SteemPay open source?
Yes, SteemPay is 100% open source. If you feel up to it, you can go through our simple and well documented codebase to see how things work! We also appreciate any contributions to the project - so check us out on GitHub!
Is SteemPay secure?
Because all transactions happen (and all passwords stored) client-side, SteemPay is very safe. That means that your passwords never leave the device, your keys are encrypted, and signing out completely wipes them from the local storage.
Why do you need my master password?
Your SteemPay wallet app needs 2 private keys (active and memo) in order to send money, and encrypt/decrypt memos. We derive these keys from the master password, encrypt them, and save them on your device for future use. The password is not saved and no longer used after this operation.
Other questions?
If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us at help@steempay.org
